Photographer

The power to turn an idea to reality is what I love the most with photography. I shoot everything from portraits, street and to fashion photography. This website is to make you as a viewer to see from my viewpoint. In my gallery you'll get to see my black and white "WHAT YEAR?" project, which shows my apprectiation for vintage.
